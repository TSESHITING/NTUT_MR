# 👁️ Through Their Eyes: An MR Experience for Learning Disabilities

[![Unity](https://img.shields.io/badge/Unity-2022.3+-blue.svg?style=flat&logo=unity)](https://unity.com/)

> **"They are not lazy, nor are they unfocused. They just see the world through a different lens."**

A Mixed Reality (MR) empathy-driven project built with **Unity** for **Meta Quest 3**. This application utilizes **MR Passthrough** and image/object detection to simulate how children with learning disabilities (such as dyslexia) perceive written text—helping educators, parents, and the public experience their daily struggles firsthand to eliminate misunderstanding and bullying.

---

## 💡 The Story & Inspiration

Many children with learning disabilities are often misunderstood by teachers and peers as being "lazy," "unfocused," or "unwilling to learn." This stigma frequently leads to academic frustration and school bullying. 

In reality, their brain processes visual information differently. To raise social awareness, this project simulates their visual reality:
*   **Jumping Text:** Words and letters shift positions erratically.
*   **Inverted & Mirrored Imagery:** Text appears upside down or backward.
*   **Blurry Vision:** Words dissolve and lose clarity, making reading an exhausting battle.

By placing users in their shoes through immersive Mixed Reality, we hope to transform judgment into empathy.

---

## 🛠️ Core Features & Technical Stack

*   **Meta Quest 3 Passthrough Integration:** Blends the user's real-world environment seamlessly with virtual interactive elements.
*   **Real-time Object/Image Detection:** Once the camera detects specific real-world triggers, a dynamic **Unity Prefab** is instantiated instantly in the user's physical space.
*   **Visual Impairment Shaders & Scripts:** Custom Unity scripts/shaders applied to text meshes to simulate dynamic text jumping, inversion, and blurring in real-time.

# 👁️ 透過他們的眼睛：學障兒童的 MR 共情體驗 (Through Their Eyes)

> **「他們不是懶惰，也不是不專心。他們只是透過不同的鏡頭在看這個世界。」**

這是一款專為 **Meta Quest 3** 開發的混合實境（MR）共情體驗專案，基於 **Unity** 引擎打造。本應用透過 **MR Passthrough（混合實境穿透）** 與圖像/物件偵測技術，真實模擬學習障礙兒童（如閱讀障礙）眼中所看到的文字世界——旨在幫助教師、家長和社會大眾親身體驗他們的日常掙扎，從而消除誤解、減少校園霸凌。

---

## 💡 專案故事與靈感來源

許多患有學習障礙的孩子，常被老師或同學誤解為「懶惰」、「不認真」或「故意不專心」，這種標籤往往導致孩子課業挫折，甚至引發嚴重的校園霸凌。

但事實上，他們的大腦大腦在處理視覺資訊時與我們不同。為了喚起社會的同理心，本專案將他們眼中的真實視覺視覺化：
*   **跳動的文字：** 單字與字母會無規則地漂移、跳動。
*   **顛倒與鏡像：** 文字會上下顛倒或前後反轉。
*   **模糊與融化：** 字體邊緣變得模糊、甚至像融化一樣難以辨識，讓閱讀變成一場精疲力竭的戰鬥。

透過 MR 沉浸式體驗，我們希望將社會的「指責」轉化為「理解與包容」。

---

## 🛠️ 核心功能與技術棧

*   **Meta Quest 3 Passthrough 整合：** 將使用者的真實物理環境與虛擬互動元素天衣無縫地結合。
*   **即時物件/圖像偵測：** 當 Quest 3 相機偵測到特定的現實世界物件時，會立即在物理空間中生成（Instantiate）相對應的 **Unity Prefab**。
*   **動態視覺混淆腳本/著色器：** 撰寫自訂的 Unity 腳本與 Shader 應用於文字組件上，動態模擬文字跳動、反轉與模糊的實時視覺效果。
